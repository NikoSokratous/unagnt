# Pricing (Commercial Tiers)

Unagnt core is **open source (MIT)**. Commercial tiers add support, SLAs, and optional hosted/enterprise features.

## Tiers (Overview)

| Tier | Use case | Support | Pricing |
|------|----------|---------|---------|
| **Community** | Self-hosted OSS | GitHub, best effort | Free |
| **Pro** | Teams needing guaranteed response | Email, private Slack, 2-business-day SLA | Contact sales |
| **Enterprise** | Large / regulated / air-gapped | Dedicated contact, SOW, runbooks, 1-day or 24/7 SLA | Contact sales |

## What’s included

- **Community**: Full OSS feature set (policy engine, audit export, workflow marketplace, cost tracking, air-gapped deploy). No warranty or SLA.
- **Pro**: Same as Community plus support channel and SLA. Optional: Compliance Pack packaging, SSO guidance.
- **Enterprise**: Same as Pro plus professional services (implementation, training, custom integrations), optional hosted tier and data residency.
